 Story 2. A Talkative Boy Do you study English? Yes, I do. I study English too. Where do you study? I study in the library. It is next to the park. I run in the park. I play tennis too. Do you watch tennis? No. I watch basketball. Do you play video games? No, I don't. I go to school at 8. I get up at 7. What time do you get up? I get up at 5. You have a nice hat. Where do you work? I work in the station. Goodbye.
