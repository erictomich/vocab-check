 G. 101 He was not at the museum. He was at the station. He was waiting for his friend. Were you at the party last night? No, I wasn't. I had a bad cold yesterday. The animals and Alice swam to shore. All the animals were safe, but Alice didn't see the rabbit. Did you go to the drugstore? No, I didn't have enough time. I'll go there tomorrow. Bob stayed up late last night. Did he get up early this morning? No, he got up at 10.
